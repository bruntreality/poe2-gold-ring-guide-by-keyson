Fractured Cold Gold Ring Craft

This is the visual checklist for the Keyson-style Gold Ring craft from your transcript. It teaches the method: buy a fractured cold base, force resistance suffixes, use Breach quality and catalysts to weight a second resistance, strand 40% attack quality, then use blocker plus Whittling loops to chase attack prefixes.

Budget warning: this is not a cheap beginner craft. The video itself shows a lucky-looking attempt that still may only break even. Paper-price the whole craft before spending.
Target Ring Shape
Base: Gold Ring, item level 82+
Locked prefix: fractured T1 added Cold Damage to Attacks
Suffixes: two T1 elemental resists, then rarity, all res, chaos res, or third res
Other prefixes: T1/T2 flat physical, lightning, or fire to attacks
Final quality: 40% Attack modifiers from Reaver Catalysts

Buy The Base

Start with item level 82+ Gold Ring and fractured T1 cold damage to attacks.

Do not pay premium for a low T1 fractured roll unless the market discount is huge. The fractured value is locked.

Best base: fractured cold plus either open mods or one T1 elemental resistance suffix.

Add Max Quality

Add a disposable prefix, then use Sinistral Crystallisation plus Essence of the Breach.

  1. Add junk prefix if needed.
  2. Activate Omen of Sinistral Crystallisation.
  3. Use Essence of the Breach.
  4. Confirm +20% maximum quality was added.
The junk prefix is there so the essence removes something you do not care about.

Slam Second Res

Use catalyst weighting plus a suffix-only Perfect Exalt to chase the second T1 resistance.

Keyson correction: the miss-recovery annul in this phase is naked. The omen annul is later for removing the Breach max-quality mod.

  1. Apply Xoph's Catalyst for fire or Esh's Catalyst for lightning.
  2. Avoid Tul's/cold catalyst for this version.
  3. Activate Omen of Dextral Exaltation.
  4. Activate Omen of Catalysing Exaltation.
  5. Use Perfect Exalted Orb.
If it misses, use a naked Orb of Annulment to try to remove the bad suffix. Do not use Dextral Annulment here. Success means the bad suffix is removed while the T1 res and Breach max-quality mod stay. If you delete either of those, backtrack.

Optional Flux

If you want chaos res, decide before you accidentally convert too many res mods.

Single chaos res

Flux the first elemental res before second res slam.

Double chaos res

Flux after two elemental res mods only if that market pays.

Unsure

Skip Flux and sell generic res rings.

6

Strand 40% Attack Quality

This is the key click sequence.

  1. Apply Reaver Catalysts until the ring is 40% attack quality.
  2. Only then activate Omen of Sinistral Annulment.
  3. Use Orb of Annulment to remove the +20% maximum-quality prefix.
Do not reverse this. If you remove the max-quality mod before Reaver quality is 40%, you lose the special quality setup.

Set Mana Blocker

Use the Collarbone / Omen of Light blocker setup from the video.

  1. Use the prefix-only preserved-bone setup on the ring.
  2. Look for the high-tier mana blocker the video wants.
  3. If the blocker is wrong, remove it with Omen of Light plus Annul and retry.
The blocker is not there for value. It is there to make Whittling behave better.

Whittle Loop

Fill the item, then use Omen of Whittling and tiered Chaos to replace low-level bad mods.

  1. Fill empty slots using the Perfect Exalt setup.
  2. Activate Omen of Whittling before the Chaos click.
  3. Use Perfect Chaos when working prefixes.
  4. Use Greater Chaos when working suffixes.
  5. Repeat until the ring has sellable attack flats and a sellable final suffix.

Remove Blocker And Sell

When the ring is marketable, remove the temporary blocker and stop.

  1. Use Omen of Light plus Annul to remove the blocker.
  2. Price check before any extra gamble.
  3. Compare by base, fractured cold roll, attack quality, attack flats, rarity, all res, and total res.
The profit comes from stopping on sellable outcomes, not from forcing a perfect six-line fantasy every time.

Stop Points

Safe stopCold + two T1 res + 40% attack quality
Good stopAdd one T1/T2 attack flat
Premium stopAdd two attack flats plus rarity/all res
Do not chaseMore clicks when comps do not justify it
